[@kiddieridesbk](https://twitter.com/kiddieridesbk) is an [open source](Not open source.) Twitter bot created by [Rustle Works](https://twitter.com/rustleworks.com). 

@kiddieridesbk places images of kiddie rides, taken around Brooklyn, into historical photos from the public domain. Images photographed and/or selected by Betsy Nagler; bot development and design by Damon Holzborn.
